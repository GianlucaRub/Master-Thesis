This folder contains the notebooks used for visualization of the results, in particular the notebook used for creating the pictures used for the final version of the thesis is "Gianluca - Results Visualization Alibaba names".
The other folders contain the notebooks used for training and testing, in particular:
- "All data" folder contains the notebooks used for training and testing and the dataset formatted as tsv files;
- "AnyBURL-23-1-sources" cointains the notebooks used for training and testing of AnyBURL only;
- "Sampling" contains the notebooks and the data used for the Dynamic Sampling simulation;
- "Subset 0" contains the notebooks and the dataset used for training and testing on one subset of the data which accounted for 29% of the entire dataset.
